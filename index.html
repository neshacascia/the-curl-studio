Welcome to The Curl Studio

Our Goal is High Quality Salon Services in The GTA

Searching for a local salon that values exceptional client experience? Look no further!

The Curl Studio isn't your typical salon. We offer VIP hair care services, with our Certified DevaCurl Stylists. Our staff are highly trained with diversity in all aspects of hair. From the straightest wave to the kinkiest curl, our goal is to exceed your expectations. Whether you have wavy, curly, or coily hair, we make sure to bring out the best of your inner curls.

Our vision is to continue making a difference in the lives of all curl types and textures, one curl at a time.

Our Services

Curl-Do

This service includes wash, conditon and style curly. Depending on your chosen service, we can arrange your hair using rods, rollers or twists. You can sit back and relax, knowing that your hair is in capable hands.

Curl Cut

Redefine your curls with our dry-cutting techniques. Our Certified DevaCut Stylists tailor each individual cut to each unique curl. We ask that you arrive with your curls naturally styled to ensure we can dry cut accordingly.

Curl Colour

Ready to spruce up your hair colour? Our professional stylists can help you achieve your hair colour dreams. We use a NO ammonia-based colour line which is formulated to maintain hair integrity and strength.

Curl Treatments

Sometimes our curls need some extra TLC! If your curls seem limp, dry, or experiencing breakage, our VIP hair treatment can be the fix. Our stylists can revive your hair and provide plenty of hair care tips to maintain your results.
